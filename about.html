I'm originally from South America and lived for several years in the United States before relocating with my husband and daughter to Sweden in 2019. Is my husband Scandinavian? Nope. So what are we doing in Sweden? Having fika, grumbling about the dark winter days, grappling with the Swedish language, and longing for the amazing summer months.

My professional life is also a bit of a smörgåsbord – I've been a student advisor, translator, paralegal and associate. In all of those roles, I've focused on helping others navigate and understand specialized content, processes and regulations. You can find more details about my work experience on my LinkedIn profile.

In my spare time, I am usually learning a new language – or two (Swedish and JavaScript at the moment), or sketching scenes from around Stockholm. I've made a gallery with some of my illustrations on my Projects page, and you can find my most recent work on Instagram.
